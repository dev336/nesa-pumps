ABOUT US



Nesa Pumps is renowned as the foremost manufacturer, supplier and exporter of submersible pumps in India, provides exclusive fluid handling solutions for residential, industrial, and commercial dewatering requirement. The comprehensive range of our submersible pumps is specifically designed in order to generate the required amount of pressure with the minimal energy usage, and is also being well-known for superb engineering, reliability, high quality standards as well as high performance all through the life span.

Today, Nesa Pumps leads the industry with a comprehensive product range in the industry that ensures quality, safety and reliability. The team of Nesa Pumps is equally zealous to offer customized fluid handling solutions to the variegated segments, incorporating agriculture, mining, residential, commercial, civil and industrial at the most competitive rates in the market.
